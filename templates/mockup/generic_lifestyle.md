# Generic Lifestyle Ecommerce Mockup

Template for any product type. Adapts dynamically based on product name, color, print design, and user's scene description.

## Prompt for AI Scene Generator

Create a premium lifestyle product mockup photograph that looks like a real Amazon/Etsy listing image.

**Product Context:**
- Product: {product_name}
- Color: {color_name}
- Print design visible on product front

**Scene Direction:**
{user_scene}

**Style Requirements:**
- Professional product photography style
- Cinematic studio lighting with soft rim light
- Warm highlights with cool shadow tones
- Realistic textures and materials
- Shallow depth of field with blurred background
- Premium ecommerce branding aesthetic

**Product Placement:**
- Product is the hero focal point, centered or positioned prominently
- Front print area visible and unobstructed
- Realistic shadows, reflections, and depth
- No distortion or alteration of the product shape

**Environment (adapt to product niche):**
- Relevant setting props that match the product category
- Clean, uncluttered background
- Surface/table/floor texture appropriate for product type

**Typography/Overlay Area (leave clear on product front):**
- Leave front print area completely clear for design composite
- Any text overlays should be placed in negative space only

**Quality:**
- Ultra realistic, photorealistic
- 8K detail, sharp focus
- Professional product photography
- Clean composition

**Restrictions:**
- NO real brand logos
- NO celebrity faces
- NO text/watermark on product itself
- DO NOT redesign or alter the product artwork
- Product must be recognizable and true to original

**Output:**
- 1:1 square composition
- Minimum 2048x2048 pixels
